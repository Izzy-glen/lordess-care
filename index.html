Our Services


Personal Hygiene & Grooming

We are well trained in personal hyegine & grooming usingextra care and only the best medically approved sanitary equipment.

Vocational Habilitation & Socialization

We assist you to create new acquaintances and make friends with new people & also acompany you to public functions.

Nature Walks & Strolls

We assist you on strolls becaus the best remedy for those who are afraid, lonely, or unhappy is to go outside, somewhere where they can be quite alone with the heavens, nature & God.

Never Miss an Appointment

We are charged with taking you to all ambulatory services, doctor, social & professional appointments.

State Travels, Vacations & Carnivals

We make sure you never miss out on the fun at every festival & also fully enjoy every vacation.

Housekeeping & Domestic Skills

We take charge of keeping your home clean and organised creating the perfecr evironment for healthy living.

Meal Planning & Preparations

We are very knowledgeable in nutrition and healthy feeding & a vast knowledge of recipies to make the best dishes you desire.

Leisure Activities, Sports & Recreation

We make sure to share in your intrests so you do not miss out on even one leisure activity. Never miss a single Buckeyes game.

Intensive Care, Love and Concern

At Lordess care we believe that time is generally the best doctor. So we provide you with patient love and care for as long as required.

Note

Our Services and resposibilities vary from person to person as specified by their Individual Service Plan (ISP).
